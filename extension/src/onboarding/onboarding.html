Protect your data before sharing with AI

Prompt Sanitizer automatically detects sensitive information — SINs, student numbers, emails, health cards, and more — before you paste text into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

Built by and for the University of Toronto community. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data ever leaves your device.

How it works

1
Paste your text

Copy text you want to send to an AI tool and open the extension popup.

2
Review detections

Sensitive items are highlighted with explanations of why they matter.

3
Sanitize & paste

One click replaces PII with safe placeholders. The AI still understands your request.
